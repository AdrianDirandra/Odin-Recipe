Rendang

Rendang is Minangkabau delicacy that is popular Indonesia. It is readily available in every Padang Restaurant in Indonesia and even many Southeast countries. Rendang popularity makes them listed on World's 50 Most Delicious Foods by CNN. Rendang taste is indeed tasty

Rendang main ingredient are meat, coconut cream, and spices such as ginger, turmeric, and lemongrass. The meat and other ingredient will be cooked in low heat for long period of time until it turned golden. This is called Rendang Kalio. Because of how it was cooked, rendang are tender and long lasting

Ingredient

  • coconut cream
  • meat
  • bay leaf
  • turmeric leaf
  • lime leaf
  • asam kandis
  • brown sugar
  • lemongrass
  • red chili
  • garlic
  • red onion
  • turmeric
  • galangal
  • ginger

Step

  1. cut the beef into rouchly 4 cm cube
  2. blend red onion, garlic, red chili, turmeric, galangal, and ginger using food processor or blender.
  3. remove the green section of the lemongrass then bash them
  4. heat some oil in the pan
  5. saute the blend of ingredient until it smells good
  6. put the meat into the pan. Keep steering the meat
  7. put the coconut cream into the pan. Now the pan should always be stirred
  8. Once it is boiled, continue to simmer over low heat until it is brown
  9. turn of the heat once it turn brown

The rendang is done cooking. Served with rice or bread
