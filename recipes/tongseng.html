Tongseng

Tongseng is a type of Javanese stew made with aromatic spices, herbs and meat like goat, mutton, lamb, beef or even chicken. Now we are going to cook beef tongseng.

Ingredient

  • beef meat
  • cooking oil
  • coconut cream
  • shredded cabbage leaves
  • sweet soy sauce
  • salt and sugar
  • shallots
  • green chili
  • tomatoes
  • cumin powder
  • royco bumbu kuning serbaguna
  • royco bumbu pelezat rasa ayam
  • water

Steps

  1. Smear the lamb with Royco Bumbu Kuning Serbaguna, leave it for a while
  2. Saute sliced shallot until the smells rouse, adds the lamb. Saut√© them until halfly cooked
  3. Pour the water and coconut milk and boil them in medium heat
  4. Add green chili, cabbage, tomatoes, and cumin. Mix them well
  5. Add Royco Royco Bumbu Pelezat Rasa Ayam and Kecap Bango. Lift and serve

The Tongseng is done cooking
